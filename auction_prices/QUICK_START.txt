STEP 1: TEST AUCTION PRICES
----------------------------

Double-click: test_auction_prices.bat

This will:
  ✓ Backup work\BLAZE.ALL automatically
  ✓ Change 3 auction prices (Healing Potion, Shortsword, Leather Armor)
  ✓ Patch into work\Blaze & Blade - Patched.bin


STEP 2: TEST IN EMULATOR
-------------------------

1. Load: work\Blaze & Blade - Patched.bin in your PS1 emulator
2. Go to the auction
3. Check if prices changed:
   - Healing Potion: 10 → 99
   - Shortsword:     22 → 88
   - Leather Armor:  36 → 77


STEP 3: RESTORE IF NEEDED
--------------------------

If something goes wrong, double-click: restore_original.bat

This will restore work\BLAZE.ALL from the most recent backup.


WHAT'S MODIFIED:
----------------

Location: 0x002EA500 in work\BLAZE.ALL
Format:   16-bit little-endian words

Word[0]  → Healing Potion (10 → 99)
Word[2]  → Shortsword     (22 → 88)
Word[13] → Leather Armor  (36 → 77)

-----------

INPUT:
  work\BLAZE.ALL                    - Game data file
  work\Blaze & Blade - Patched.bin  - Game disc image

OUTPUT:
  work\BLAZE.ALL.backup_*           - Automatic backups
  work\Blaze & Blade - Patched.bin  - Patched disc (overwritten)
